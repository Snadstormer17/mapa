Obristan

Obristan is a militaristic industrial nation located in the northwest of the continent. Known for its strict border policies, harsh winters, and industrial production, it plays a major role in regional politics. The government is highly centralized, with power held by a Supreme Leader and a tight circle of advisors. Most trade routes into Obristan are monitored by military checkpoints, and travelers must present proper documentation at all times.

Geography

Obristan’s northern border is marked by tundra and mountain ranges, while dense forests fill its southern valleys. The capital, Skal, is a major logistical hub connected by extensive rail infrastructure.

Politics

Obristan is governed through a highly centralized autocracy. Elections are rare and tightly controlled. Most civil services are operated by the military or party elites, with strong propaganda influences over media.

Economy

Obristan’s economy is focused on heavy industry, particularly in weapons manufacturing, mining, and chemicals. Despite international sanctions, it maintains a robust internal supply chain.
